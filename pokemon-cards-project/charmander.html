Charmander

The flame that burns at the tip of its tail is an indication of its emotions. The flame wavers when Charmander is enjoying itself. If the Pokémon becomes enraged, the flame burns fiercely.

  • Category: Lizard
  • Abilities: Blaze
  • Type: Fire
  • Weaknesses: Water, Ground, Rock

A bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with a primarily orange body and blue. A fire burns at the tip of this Pokémon's slender tail and has blazed there since Charmander's birth.
